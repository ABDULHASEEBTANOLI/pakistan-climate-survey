Pakistan Climate Change Survey 2026 — PIEAS National Research Initiative

Study Title

Climate Change Perception, Impact Assessment, and Policy Priorities in Pakistan — A National Survey (2026)

Purpose

This research aims to document lived experience and expert knowledge of climate change impacts across all provinces of Pakistan. Data will be used for academic publication and evidence-based policy recommendations.

What We Collect

Name, email, phone number, location (province/district), professional background, and your perceptions/observations about climate change. No sensitive financial or biometric data is collected.

How Your Data Will Be Used

Aggregated and anonymized data will be used for academic research, journal publication, and policy recommendation reports. Individual responses will NOT be shared publicly or sold to any third party.

Voluntary Participation

Participation is entirely voluntary. You may stop at any time. There are no negative consequences for declining or stopping.

Contact

For questions about this research, contact: — Department of Metallurgy & Materials Engineering, PIEAS, Islamabad.
